# Motion Prompts (Veo via Flow) — the 6 scroll-scrubbed "Divine Power" reveals

These are the centrepiece clips: **MG-F1..F6**. Each form is a **pinned, full-height chapter** where the
**scroll position becomes the clip's playhead** (Apple-style) so the deity performs a *powerful divine act*
exactly as fast as the user scrolls. All are reverent and **never gory** — power shown as light, force and
protection. Wiring details: [`../docs/production-plan.md`](../docs/production-plan.md) §6.5.

Each clip **starts from its forced-perspective hero still** (the matching `image-prompts.md` output) and
opens out into motion, keeping the same **pure black void + single theme-coloured rim light**.

## Workflow (all six)
- **Frames to Video** with the form's still as the **start frame**; add `IMG-00` as an **Ingredient** for face consistency.
- **Length:** 8s (extend in Scene Builder if you want a longer scrub). Source motion can be bold — the scroll controls speed.
- **Aspect:** 16:9 (pinned chapter). Export highest resolution; keep raw in `_raw/`.
- **Append the NEGATIVE** to every prompt:
  ```
  no on-screen text, no captions, no watermark, no logos, no second figure, no background scenery,
  no modern objects, no rifle, no gun, no gore, no blood, no real injury, no distorted hands,
  no extra fingers, no melted features, no cartoon look, no bright daylight, no clutter.
  ```

## Master Style tail (paste at the end of each prompt; swap the colour words per form)
```
STYLE: cinematic devotional, pure near-black void (#0A0A0B), a single directional rim light in the form's
theme colour, high-contrast chiaroscuro, volumetric smoke, floating grey ash and drifting theme-coloured
sparks. Photoreal-mythic, sculptural dark bronze-black idol, reverent and fierce — never gory. Shallow depth
of field, anamorphic feel, fine film grain, gentle vignette. 16:9.
```

## MG-F1 — Sangili Karuppan · breaks his chains (Fire-Red / Ember `#C1272D → #FF6B1A`)
**Start frame:** Image 0 still · **Where:** `/forms` chapter 1

> Open on the forced-perspective hero frame of the chain-bound guardian idol, lone in the black void. As the scroll advances, the **anamorphic low-angle camera eases back from the bare foot to reveal his full towering form**. His muscles strain and the **heavy iron chains binding his chest and arms creak, pull taut, then SNAP and burst outward in a shockwave of embers and sparks**; as the links shatter, an **expanding ring of protective fire-red flame erupts from his body and seals the surrounding darkness away**. His **eyes blaze fire-red**, smoke billows up through the firelight. Slow build into a powerful release. Lighting: ember-orange key (`#FF6B1A`) with deep fire-red glints (`#C1272D`) on the chains and eyes, blood-deep shadows, pure black void. Mood: fierce, liberating, protective. AUDIO: chains groaning then snapping, a deep resonant boom, rising devotional drone, soft fire crackle. *(append the Master Style tail, fire-red/ember.)*
