# Still Prompts (Imagen via Flow) — the 6 Forms

Long-narrative, forced-perspective hero stills. Each preserves the concept — **extreme low-angle from the
bare front-facing right foot up to the face, a lone figure in a pure black void** — and expands it with full
material, lighting, camera, atmosphere and theme-colour detail.

**For every prompt below:**
- Feed the matching **`image_X.png`** (and `IMG-00` master) as **Ingredients** so the face/ornaments stay identical.
- Keep the dark upper corners free for the code-overlaid Tamil/English type.
- Generate **4:5** (forms card) **and** a **16:9** version.
- **Append this NEGATIVE:**
  ```
  no on-screen text, no captions, no watermark, no logos, no second figure, no background scenery,
  no modern objects, no rifle, no gun, no gore, no blood, no distorted hands, no extra fingers,
  no melted features, no cartoon look, no bright daylight, no clutter.
  ```
